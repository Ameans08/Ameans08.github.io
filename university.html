Mechanical Engineering

Mechanical engineers play a vital role in designing, analyzing, and manufacturing mechanical systems and devices. Typically requiring a bachelor's degree in mechanical engineering, this field of study covers a diverse range of subjects, including thermodynamics, fluid mechanics, and materials science. Graduates often enter the workforce with a solid understanding of mechanical principles, and the median annual salary for mechanical engineers is around $90,160, making it a financially rewarding career choice.
